Enthusiasts Acquiring and Sharing Technical Knowledge 

Wants You!

  1. Do you live on the east side of the metro area?
  2. Are you interested or working in any areas of high technology?
  3. Do you want to meet others and share your interests & knowledge?

Then EAST Knowledge wants you! EAST Knowledge is looking for people interested or working in any area of technology to get together for stimulating presentations and conversations.

We will provide a forum where technically-minded individuals on the east side can regularly meet to share ideas, knowledge, and skills and promote camaraderie. The emphasis is on:

  1. Inventiveness
  2. Inquisitiveness
  3. Sharing what we know
  4. Promoting a “Culture of Cooperation” on the east side

Everyone is invited to participate. Whether you’re just getting started or you’ve been at it for a while, we want to hear from you. We may be geographically challenged, but together we can form something great!

We will hold regular meetings with presentations which are followed by general mingling. The idea is to have peers talking to each other, asking questions, and sharing what they know.

Please send us an email indicating your interest.
